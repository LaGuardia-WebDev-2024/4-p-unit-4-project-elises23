Best Food Places in Jamaica! (Queens)

Breakfast...yum

Abu Musa Deli & Grill is your best friend when it comes to breakfast on the go. They have everyting you could imagine which you can acquire with a quickness.

  • Bacon Egg and Cheese
  • Bagels
  • Coffee


Lunch...yum

3 Kingz Pizza shop has the best pizza there. All types of pizza for really cheap. $4 for two slices whatever flavor. Come check it out!

  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese
  • Chicken and BBQ


Dinner...yummy

The Door is one of the best Jamaican restaurants out there. It'll make you feel like you're really in Jamaica once you bite into that jerk chicken with baked mac and cheese and some collard greens.

  • Jerk Chicken
  • Mac and Cheese
  • Collard Greens
